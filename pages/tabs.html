Introduction.

Using sales evaluation from sales reports, the projects delivers a decision-making tool through demand forecasting and company performance. The business goal is first anticipate future products marketing based on the type of marketing, the product, and the demographic characteristics of the consumer, and then to manage the company performance.

Problem Statement

Most Small scale businesses have the challenge to monitor the performance of their businesses and thus they are unable to evaluate their business. Most of them keep the records of their businesses manually and thus they are not able to track their sales. This system will help to address this problem by automating the process of sales evaluation.

Problem Statement cont.

Small scale businesses are unable to identify their target market. Using information about customer history of purchased products and behavior, the system will help the business owners in choosing the right marketing message for the right customer and also help to determine which product to be sold to which group.

General Objectives

To develop a system based on customers purchase history which will help in performance evaluation and can be utilized in any small scale firm to aid in decision-making and improve marketing.

Proporsed System

This is a user-friendly system for small scale businesses e.g. grocery shops, that helps in sales performance calculation. Also, the system help small scale business in decision making and manage their stock.

Justification

The sales performance system solves the problem of sales evaluation and acts as an effective decision-making tool. Assessing the performance of salespeople includes allocating a relative score to reflect a business performance on the identified dimension for measuring both sales performance and behavioral aspects of the employee in the job. A performance evaluation must have to link with the strategic mission of the sales force and the performance standard taken for measurement, it should be specific, measurable, attainable, and realistic and time specific which reflect the quality of the business how well the employee performs on the expected terms of the sales objective.

Clear Slide

Clear Slide is a software, which helps businesses to manage the company’s customerfacing content and access and present it as needed. For businesses whose products have a longer and interactive sales cycle, this tool allows them to get rid to the right content quickly, and get it at their prospect without having the message in the head hence the software helps to automatically present the content. However, the Clear Slide does not provide analytics about the customer’s content reviews and what they like about a product to help in market segmentation.
